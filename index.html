01

Problem

Metro information can be fragmented, connectivity is not guaranteed underground, and a trip often requires more context than a static map.

02

Product

MetroYar combines offline station-to-station routing, nearby discovery, maps, timetable context, favorites, and sharing in one focused Android experience.

03

Engineering

Jetpack Compose and Clean Architecture keep product iteration structured while an offline-first flow protects the core journey from network availability.

04

Outcome

An independently released product with 47K Bazaar installs and 14K Myket downloads, maintained through iterative store releases.
